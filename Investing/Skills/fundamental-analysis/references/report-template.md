# Report Template

Use this exact structure. Keep the whole report readable in one sitting —
aim for the length of a good newsletter issue, not a 10-K.

---

### 💡 The key insight about this business

[The single most important thing a beginner must understand about HOW this

### Buy-below price

[Show the math in three visible steps: (1) normalized EPS or FCF/share —
mid-cycle for cyclicals; (2) a fair multiple justified by the company's
own 5-yr median and its quality tier (15–20x steady quality, 20–25x only
for moat 12+ and ROIC >20%, 10–14x slow growers and cyclicals);
(3) a margin of safety — 10% for scores 80+, 15% for 65–79, 20% for
50–64. State the resulting round number and how far the current price is
from it. For scores under 50, explain in one sentence why no entry price
fixes the thesis. This is a rubric-consistent value estimate, not a
prediction of where the stock will trade.]
